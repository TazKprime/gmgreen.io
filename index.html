Yield Estimation Methodology
Step 1Object detection model counts fruits per stage (flower, immature, mature, ripe, spoilt) per drone image tile
Step 2Fruit-set ratio = ripe÷(ripe+immature+mature). Flowering density predicts potential yield ±15%
Step 3NDVI from Sentinel-2 adjusts baseline: NDVI <0.4 = stress penalty, NDVI >0.7 = premium
Step 4Density × avg fruit weight × harvestable fraction = estimated kg/acre
Step 5Claude API (web_search) fetches current MoFA Ghana benchmarks for calibration
